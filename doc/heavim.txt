1. KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS ~ 				   *heavim-shortcuts*

Note: Heavim defines Space as the global leader key, and comma (',') as the
local leader key.

   +                    Toggle folds

   <Leader><Space>      Clear highlighted search
   <Leader>M            Toggle mouse
   <Leader>T		Toggle visible tabs
